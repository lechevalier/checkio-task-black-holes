You have to help Stephen to implement software model (function) that predicts state of black holes under control. A&A research team has identified some peculiarities in the behavior of black holes.

To create software model one should take into account:

1. Cartesian coordinate system is used.
2. Each black hole is represented as a circle with x, y (coordinates) and r (radius).
3. In contrast to the area, which may change during the absorption process, the coordinates remain constant.
4. Area of black hole greatly influences on its mass, hence gravity field.
5. The absorption process of black holes depends on distance between their centers - starting from the minimal. If the distance between different black holes is equal then first pair should be taken into account.
6. The absorption process (merging) of black holes possible if and only if two conditions are met:
   - Intersection area of one of two black holes is greater or equal 55% (>= 55%) of its current area.
   - The area of one of two black holes is dominated over 20% (>= 20%).
7. If one black hole absorbs another one their areas are summarized (Stotal = S1 + S2).
8. The absorption process continues as long as all conditions are met (see p. 6).
